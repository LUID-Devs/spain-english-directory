## Valencia Opportunity Analysis

**Expat Info Desk does NOT have a Valencia city guide.** This represents a significant first-mover opportunity for our directory.

### Why Valencia is Important:

1. **Third-largest city in Spain** after Madrid and Barcelona
2. **Growing expat population** - particularly among digital nomads and retirees
3. **Lower cost of living** than Barcelona/Madrid attracts budget-conscious expats
4. **Strong tech scene** - home to emerging startup ecosystem
5. **No dedicated English-language directory** currently dominates the market

### Recommended Valencia Categories to Develop:

1. Healthcare providers (public and private)
2. International schools (British, American, French)
3. Real estate agents specializing in expat relocations
4. Immigration lawyers and tax advisors
5. Coworking spaces for digital nomads
6. Expat social groups and networking events
7. English-speaking service providers (dentists, vets, etc.)
