# Expat Info Desk - Competitor Analysis

**Analysis Date:** March 6, 2026  
**Website:** https://www.expatinfodesk.com  
**Focus:** English-speaking services for expats in Spain

## Executive Summary

Expat Info Desk is a relocation guide platform offering city-specific guides for expats. They provide general relocation information but have significant gaps in their service provider directory coverage, particularly for Valencia and professional service listings.

### Key Finding: Valencia Gap
**Expat Info Desk has NO dedicated Valencia city guide.** Valencia is only mentioned briefly in their general Spain overview page, despite being Spain's third-largest city and a major expat destination. This represents a significant content gap we can exploit.
