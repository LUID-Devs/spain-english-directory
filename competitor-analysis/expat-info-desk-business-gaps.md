## Data Quality Observations

### Expat Info Desk Listing Quality

**Strengths:**
- Includes languages spoken
- Addresses with postal codes
- Phone numbers (though some may be outdated)
- Some email addresses
- Organized by specialty

**Weaknesses:**
- Many listings lack websites
- No verification dates
- No user reviews
- Limited description of services
- Static content (not updated frequently)

### How We Can Differentiate

1. **Verification Status** - Confirm businesses still exist and speak English
2. **Richer Descriptions** - Services offered, specialties, prices
3. **User Reviews** - Community feedback
4. **Photos** - Clinic/practice images
5. **Online Booking** - Direct appointment scheduling
6. **Current Contact Info** - Verified phone/email/website

### Expat Info Desk Advantages
