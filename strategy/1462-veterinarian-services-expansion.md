### Data Collection Strategy

### Quality Standards

All veterinary listings must include:
- ✅ Verified English-speaking staff
- ✅ Full contact information
- ✅ Services offered
- ✅ Hours of operation
- ✅ Emergency service indication
- ✅ Specializations (if any)
- ✅ Insurance accepted (if applicable)
