# Angloinfo Spain - Competitor Gap Analysis

**Research Date:** March 2025  
**Issue:** #1336 - Competitor Gap: Angloinfo Has 360+ Listings Per City  
**Analyst:** OpenClaw Subagent

---

## Executive Summary

Angloinfo is the largest and most established English-language expat directory for Spain, with an estimated 360+ listings per major city. They operate a franchise-like model with local sites covering major expat destinations. Their directory focuses on English-speaking, expat-friendly businesses across essential service categories.

## 5. Key Gaps & Opportunities

### Geographic Gaps (High Priority)

1. **Seville** - Andalusia's capital, no dedicated Angloinfo site
   - Population: 690,000
   - Growing expat community
   - Major business hub

2. **Granada** - Popular tourist and student city
   - Population: 233,000
   - Strong English-speaking community
   - Digital nomad destination

3. **Bilbao/San Sebastián** - Basque Country
   - Combined population: 530,000+
   - Wealthy region with international business
   - No English directory presence

4. **Secondary Cities in Andalusia:**
   - Córdoba: 325,000
   - Jaén: 112,000
   - Almería city: 200,000

### Category Gaps (Medium Priority)

1. **Tech & Digital Services:**
   - Web developers
   - Digital marketers
   - IT support
   - Co-working spaces
   - Limited coverage on Angloinfo

2. **Specialized Healthcare:**
   - Fertility clinics
   - Cosmetic surgery
   - Specialist diagnostics
   - Pediatric specialists

3. **Niche Services:**
   - Pet relocation specialists
   - International shipping
   - Translation services
   - Cross-border tax specialists

### Feature Gaps (Opportunity)

1. **Modern UX:** Angloinfo design is dated (legacy platform)
2. **Mobile Experience:** Limited mobile optimization
3. **Search Functionality:** Basic keyword search only
4. **Verification:** Limited business verification process
5. **Community Features:** No user forums or Q&A
6. **Content Freshness:** Directory updates are infrequent
7. **Photos/Videos:** Most listings lack visual content

## 6. Competitive Positioning

### Angloinfo Strengths
- ✅ Established brand recognition (20+ years)
- ✅ Large existing listing base
- ✅ Multi-city coverage
- ✅ SEO authority and domain age
- ✅ Franchise model creates local knowledge
- ✅ How-to guides and content

### Angloinfo Weaknesses
- ❌ Outdated website design and UX
- ❌ Poor mobile experience
- ❌ Limited search/filter capabilities
- ❌ Inconsistent listing quality
- ❌ Limited verification of businesses
- ❌ No real-time updates
- ❌ Missing major cities (Seville, Granada, Bilbao)

### Our Opportunities
1. **Modern, mobile-first platform**
2. **Verified business program**
3. **Enhanced search and filtering**
4. **Focus on underserved cities**
5. **Tech/startup ecosystem coverage**
6. **Community-driven reviews and Q&A**
7. **Integration with Spain English Directory network**

---

## 7. Strategic Recommendations

### Phase 1: City Selection
Focus on Angloinfo's geographic gaps:
1. **Seville** - Largest opportunity
2. **Granada** - Lower competition
3. **Bilbao** - Affluent market

### Phase 2: Category Prioritization
1. **Healthcare** - Highest search volume
2. **Legal** - Essential for new arrivals
3. **Real Estate** - High value listings
4. **Financial Services** - Repeat engagement

### Phase 3: Differentiation
1. **Verification badge system** for trusted providers
2. **Real-time availability** for appointments
3. **Community reviews** with verified user program
4. **Content integration** - directory + guides
5. **Mobile app** for on-the-go access
