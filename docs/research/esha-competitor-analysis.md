## Competitive Differentiation Strategy

### What Expatica Does Well
1. **Brand recognition** - 26 years in market
2. **SEO authority** - High domain authority
3. **Featured listings** - Clear monetization model
4. **Broad categories** - 9 major sections beyond healthcare

### Where Expatica is Weak
1. **Listing depth** - Minimal information per listing
2. **Geographic gaps** - Many cities underrepresented
3. **No verification system** - Anyone can list
4. **No user reviews** - No social proof
5. **No claim system** - Providers can't manage listings

### Our Competitive Advantages
1. **Verified listings only** - Quality over quantity
2. **Rich profiles** - Detailed descriptions, full contact info
3. **Claim workflow** - Provider ownership and updates
4. **Review system** - User-generated social proof
5. **Lead generation** - Direct contact functionality
