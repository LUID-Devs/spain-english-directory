## Verification Strategy

### English-Speaking Verification Process

To differentiate from competitor's unverified claims:

1. **Phone Verification**: Call each clinic to confirm English-speaking staff
2. **Documentation**: Request English-language capabilities from clinic owners
3. **User Reviews**: Allow expats to confirm English service quality
4. **Badge System**: Create verified English-speaking badges
