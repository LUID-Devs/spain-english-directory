> **Task:** #1365  
> **Date:** March 2025  
> **Research Status:** Complete

## Executive Summary

ESHA Spain is a **not-for-profit healthcare directory** serving the English-speaking community in Spain. While they have strong brand recognition and healthcare specialization, there are significant gaps Spain English Directory can exploit through broader service categories, superior technology, and transparent verification.

| **Mobile app** | Low | Medium | High |

### Key Takeaway

> **ESHA's healthcare-only focus is their strength AND weakness.** While they dominate healthcare search results, they cannot serve the full expat journey. Spain English Directory should position as the **complete expat services platform** - healthcare, legal, tax, real estate, and more - with superior technology and transparent verification.
