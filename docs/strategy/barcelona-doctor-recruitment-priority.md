## English Proficiency Verification Process

### Self-Reported Levels

| Level | Description | Verification Method |
|-------|-------------|---------------------|
| **B1** | Basic communication | Phone screening (10 min) |
| **B2** | Independent user | Video interview (15 min) |
| **C1** | Proficient | Video interview + reference check |
| **C2** | Mastery | Video interview + English certification review |
| **Native** | Native speaker | Verification of education/work in English-speaking country |

### Assessment Questions

**B1 Level Check:**
- "Please describe your medical specialty in English."
- "How would you explain a common diagnosis to a patient?"

**B2+ Level Check:**
- "Describe a complex medical procedure."
- "How do you handle explaining side effects of medication?"
- "Role-play: Patient asks about treatment options."

**C1+ Level Check:**
- "Explain a rare condition and treatment plan."
- "Discuss medical ethics scenario."
- "Handle a nuanced mental health consultation."
