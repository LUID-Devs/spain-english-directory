# SpainMadeSimple Competitor Analysis

## Overview

**SpainMadeSimple** (spainmadesimple.com) is a comprehensive English-language resource website focused on helping expatriates and English-speaking visitors navigate various aspects of life in Spain. The site specializes in location-specific guides and directories for essential services including healthcare, dental care, and medical professionals.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Website** | https://www.spainmadesimple.com/ |
| **Primary Language** | English |
| **Geographic Focus** | Nationwide (Spain) with city-specific pages |
| **Primary Format** | Online directory + informational guides |
| **Target Audience** | English-speaking expats, tourists, new residents |
| **Content Style** | Practical guides with business listings |

---

## Business Model

SpainMadeSimple operates as a **free-to-list directory with informational content**:

### Key Characteristics

1. **Free Business Listings**
   - Dental clinics can submit their details for free
   - No paid tier mentioned in search results
   - Submission form for new listings

2. **Geographic Organization**
   - City-specific landing pages
   - Regional grouping (Costa del Sol, Costa Blanca, etc.)
   - Island-specific pages (Tenerife, Lanzarote)

3. **Content Strategy**
   - "What to Expect" guides for each service type
   - Practical advice for foreigners
   - Emergency service information
